Fertility Rates

Now we look at the world in terms of fertility. Once again, the darker the country, the higher the rate (in this case, children per woman).

These are the countries with the highest fertility rates:

Niger, Burundi, Mali, Somalia, Uganda, Burkina Faso, Zambia, Malawi, Angola, Afghanistan, South Sudan, Mozambique, Nigeria, Myanmar, Ethiopia, Timor-Leste, Benin, Tanzania, Guinea, Sierra Leone, Cameroon, Liberia, Republic of Congo, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Equitorial Guinea
